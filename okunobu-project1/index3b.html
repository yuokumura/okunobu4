FAQ

Can I use MAJORITY if I’m not a migrant?

Yes! Everyone is welcome. If any of the features in our app interest you, sign up and give it a try.

Is MAJORITY a bank?

We are a financial membership app, and our services are provided by Sutton Bank, Member FDIC. The signature MAJORITY Visa® Debit Card is also issued by Sutton Bank pursuant to a license from Visa U.S.A. Inc. The card can be used anywhere Visa is accepted!

What is MAJORITY?

MAJORITY is the first-ever financial membership built by migrants, for migrants. After signing up, you get an FDIC-insured account with no overdraft fees, a Visa® Debit Card, fee-free money transfers, free and low-cost international calls, and more!

Where is MAJORITY?

We have offices in Houston, Miami, and Stockholm, Sweden.Visit us at MAJORITY Meetup Houston, located in 9801 V Bissonnet Street, Houston, Texas 77036, United States.

Will my money be safe with MAJORITY?

Absolutely. Your MAJORITY account is FDIC-insured, so whether you are in the US or abroad, the money in your account is protected up to $250,000 by the FDIC. Plus, you’ll be able to access your app anywhere, so there’s nothing to worry about.
